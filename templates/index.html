Dissolved Oxygen Summary

The Dissolved Oxygen Predictor uses a LightGBM machine learning model, optimized by Differential Evolution, to forecast dissolved oxygen (DO) levels in aquaculture environments.

It analyzes real-world sensor data (depth, temperature, pH, conductivity) from over 47,000 samples. With advanced preprocessing and tuning, it achieved a 97.2% prediction accuracy.

This tool helps optimize fish health and environmental management by providing reliable DO predictions in dynamic aquatic systems.
